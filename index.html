a story

Patience. Persistence. Perseverance.

2010

Dennis retired in 2010, after a profressional career of over 35 years. Determined to enjoy retirement, he had enjoyed beauty in life and in nature, so it only seemed natural to try to capture little moments of time by way of photography.

The draw of photography took hold for good with his first trip to Africa, visting Zambia, Zimbabwe, and South Africa. The lure of the wild animals 'in the wild' has lead to countless hours of enjoyment through the images he has captured. The digital images right out of the camera were not 'good enuff', so it was a necessary to try to learn the 'Art of Photography' a bit better so that the images might better represent what Dennis saw in his mind when the images were captured.

In order to learn the art, Dennis has taken numerous classes in attempt to better understand the skills involved. The formal education in photography led to a learning vacation in London and Paris as a foreign exchange student, where he was older than the parents of his fellow students. Loving every minute of the travel and capturing the moments has only served to further Dennis' interest and hopefully his skill in bringing to life what he has seen, by way of his images.

Dennis has also studied graphic design and web design and has built this site with you in mind. Please explore the images on this site for your enjoyment. This site is a work in progress and will grow as Dennis' body of images grow and also his ability to make this site better and easier to explore.
